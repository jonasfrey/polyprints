## Printability Notes
- At 40mm scale, avoid thin features (antlers, tails) under 2mm thickness
- Flat base preferred — no supports needed
- Low-poly style naturally reduces overhangs (large flat faces)
- Solid prints at this size are fast and strong
- Consider splitting complex models (e.g. antlers printed separately and glued)
