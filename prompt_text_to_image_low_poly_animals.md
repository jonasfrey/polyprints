We worked through building an image generation prompt step by step:

Refined the subject to a low-polygon animal figurine with geometric faceted surfaces and pastel matte colors.
Tried adding three orthographic views (front, side, top) in a CAD blueprint layout, but I noted that multi-view layouts are unreliable with most image generators.
Simplified to a single isometric view, which gave us the final clean prompt — a low-poly animal figurine seen from a 45-degree isometric angle, no perspective distortion, on a pure white background.
